#Music as a social art

We have to understand that Music is a social phenomenon.

Some people would agree that the appropiation of public, common knowledge and
cultural legacy of music for the purposes of policy regulation is necessary
and unavoidable if we wish to stablish practices which are healthy and
socially constructive.

We have to give back what we take from society.

If we know how to manange common needs and create a collaborative market system,
funding of high quailty shows and performances is perfectly possible.
